About Autogen

Autogen was founded in 2025 with a mission to revolutionize the food processing industry through technology. Our team of experts has deep knowledge of both software development and the unique challenges faced by food processors, from supply chain management to quality control and compliance.

We believe that technology should be an enabler, not a hurdle. Our software solutions are designed to be intuitive, scalable, and highly effective, helping our clients reduce waste, improve efficiency, and maintain the highest standards of food safety. We are committed to building long-term partnerships with our clients, helping them grow and succeed in a competitive market.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of software solutions for the global food processing industry, empowering businesses of all sizes to achieve operational excellence.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative, reliable, and user-friendly software that solves the real-world problems of food processors, backed by exceptional customer service.
